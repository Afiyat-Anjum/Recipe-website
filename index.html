Apple Cake with Caramel Sauce

Delicious Obsessions

11

Ingredients

|

55

Minerals

|

380

Calories

Ingredients

1/2 cup unsalted butter

1 cup sugar

2 eggs

1/2 cup flour

1/4 teaspoon nutmeg

1/2 teaspoon cinnamon

1 teaspoon baking soda

1/4 teaspoon salt

2 cups diced apple

1 teaspoon vanilla

1/4 cup pecans

Method

1

Preheat oven to 350 and grease baking dish.
2

Whisk dry ingredients and spices
3

Toss apples with lemon.
4

Cream sugars and butter until fluffy.
5

Mix in eggs then blend in flour mixture.
6

Mix in apples.
7

Pour batter into pan and spread even.
8

Bake until set.
9

Serve with brown sugar caramel sauce.
